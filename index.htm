The Circuit

In December 2014, DVRPC and the William Penn Foundation announced a renewed commitment to investment in the Circuit, Greater Philadelphia's Regional Trail Network. Building the Circuit, DVRPC's Regional Trails Program will benefit from a new $7 million grant from the William Penn Foundation. These funds will be used for targeted investment in the planning, design, and construction of multi-use trails in Greater Philadelphia.
